Glitch Art


Glitching art often involves finding a way of distorting an image in ways that are seemingly random and don't target a specific element of the image. Yet when tampered with in certain ways, they can give alternate meanings to original works. For this project, we messed with the internal data of different web images by loading them into programs like Notepad++, and deleted, added, and altered parts of the broken lines that showed up in these programs.
